About Me

My background is in Chemical Engineering. Throughout my previous career, I realized that I loved problem solving, engineering solutions, and working with other engineers. But I was more passionate about software and programming, so I decided to change careers and learn it. I attended General Assembly bootcamp to get that boost I needed.

Learning new languages and developing my own apps has been one of the most rewarding experiences so far. I have made multiple full stack apps using Python Django framework and the MERN stack. I also love applying machine learning in my applications, as I think machine learning and AI are going to change the world of tech. Check out my work below.
